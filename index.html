About Me

I am a senior at The Woodstock Academy. I am an Honor Roll student and take pride in the work I do. I aspire to be a developer in the InfoSec and Computer Science fields. My goal is to be a part of a Pentesting and Information Securities team for a major company.

I take inspiration from companies like Pratt and Whitney and Neuralink. I look at how they function in their respective fields and admire the duties of the specialists that work for them. Technology has been an interest of mine since a young age, and only increased as I got older. It transformed from primarily a source of entertainment to the core of my career and a staple in who I am.
